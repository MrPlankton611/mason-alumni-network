A national networking group that allows flexible ways alumni can connect within similar career fields, across companies, and within or across cities. Collaborate, network, and share opportunities.

Who: All alumni

Mentorship Program

Matching alumni early in careers with current college MHS alumni to share workplace realities, plus new-to-career alumni matched with established career alumni.

Who: Early, new-to-career, and established alumni
